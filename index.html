Move over Google! Make way Facebook! Get ready for the next big thing - hamster kimonos! You know the problem - your pet hamsters are dressed just like all the other little hamsters. Nothing to stand out or show off at all. But now, Hamster Kimonos Inc. will bring the world of haute couture to your furry friends. No more drab turtlenecks. No more holey sweaters. Instead, imagine your pet friends decked out in the finest silken kimonos. Rich patterned fabrics, with flowers and rainbows and butterfly designs. Heads will turn, jaws will drop. Your little hamsters will be adorned in elegance. Just imagine: a nice meal of cucumber, a quick workout on the treadmill. Then your pets can get dressed in silk luxury and head out for an evening of fun.

You love your hamsters. You feed them cucumber and apple, you change the water in the water bottle often to keep it full of fresh water. But still they seem bored. Listless. How to renew that joie de vivre? Imagine their surprise, when they nibble open a little box left in their cage, and behold, it contains fine silk kimonos! Of course they will want to try them on at once, and then wear them all the time afterwards. In the pictures, we can see Patches in her favorite red kimono, examining some dandelion leaves. Meanwhile Rock'o wears his red kimono with a butterfly pattern when going rock climbing. Sergeant Serge loves his floral kimono. Here he examines a water pipe. Note that none of them look like ordinary hamsters. Thanks to their elegant kimonos, they can all explore in luxury.
